# ngogohou_sidequest_W5

## Project Title

Side Quest Week 5: reflective camera experience

---

## Author

Nelly Gogohounga - ngogohou - 21106347

## Description

A reflective galaxy exploration experience where the player drifts through a world larger than the screen. The camera moves slowly and smoothly, creating a cinematic feeling of space. Hidden stars glow and planets are discovered.

## Setup

The camera follows the player .
The world includes stars, large planets (some with rings), and hidden interactive stars.

Controls: Arrow keys
